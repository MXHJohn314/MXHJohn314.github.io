About Me

By Day...

Full-stack software engineer

I have two years of experience with big data tools, mobile app development, and QA test automation, allowing me to address a variety of complex technical challenges and contribute to innovative solutions across the board. Now for the interesting part.

By Night...

Keyboard designer and ergonomic keyboard enthusiast

"What if Backspace and Capslock were swapped?" This is the question that initiated my software engineering journey years ago.

When I hurt my back in College, I was bedridden for an entire summer. As a result, I spent so much time on my computer that my fingers and wrists started to hurt. That's when I started to wonder if it was possible to change the keyboard layout on my laptop. I learned about a scripting language called AutoHotkey, which was my first coding experience. I found myself switching from electrical engineering to computer science, and loving every minute of it! I excelled in my cohort, and took a job on campus as a Learning Assistant.

Back to the keyboard geekiness - it was just getting started. Throughout my college years, I designed several iterations of ergonomic keyboards with QMK, the open-source mechanical keyboard software. I designed my first keyboard and named it Comfy Thumbs, an ergonomic, bluetooth keyboard with 6 comfortable and easy-to-reach keys on for each thumb!

But what about soft keyboards for phones? Well, it turns out I also designed Griddle - my own gesture-based keyboard, based on the popular MessagEase on-screen keyboard.

If you've read this far, you're either really into keyboards or are looking for top talent.
